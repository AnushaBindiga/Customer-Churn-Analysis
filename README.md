\# Customer Churn Analysis



\## Business Problem

A telecommunications company is experiencing high customer churn. 

This project identifies who is churning, why they are leaving, 

and provides data-driven recommendations to reduce churn.



\## Tools Used

\- \*\*SQL\*\* — Data exploration and analysis

\- \*\*Excel\*\* — Data cleaning, pivot tables and charts

\- \*\*Power BI\*\* — Interactive dashboard



\## Dataset

\- Source: Kaggle Telco Customer Churn Dataset

\- 7,043 customers

\- 20 columns including contract type, internet service, payment method, tenure and monthly charges



\## Key Findings

\- Overall churn rate is \*\*26.54%\*\* — 1 in 4 customers leaving

\- Month-to-month customers churn at \*\*42%\*\* vs \*\*3%\*\* for two year contracts

\- Fiber optic customers have the highest churn at \*\*69%\*\*

\- Electronic check customers churn at \*\*45%\*\* — highest of all payment methods

\- High value customers (charges > $70) with low tenure are highest risk



\## Recommendations

1\. Incentivise month-to-month customers to switch to annual contracts

2\. Investigate fiber optic service quality issues

3\. Encourage customers to switch from electronic check to automatic payment

4\. Create retention program targeting new customers in first 12 months
